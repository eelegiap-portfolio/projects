Education Epidemic

The Education Epidemic is an interactive website that sheds light on the barriers students face to remote learning across the United States. The data used to create the visualizations is from the Census Bureau's American Community Survey, a monthly survey that examines the living situations of families chosen at random across the US. Inspiration for the project was drawn from personal experience and a belief that not enough attention is being paid to the challenges of remote learning underscored by structural inequalities.

The challenges we explored were food insecurity, Internet access, computer access, and noise levels.

My contributions to the project mainly lie in the adaption of D3.js maps to show the percentage of students in challenging remote learning situations. The map was adapted from this map of England from ONSVisual on GitHub to a map showing the United States along with a corresponding bar chart. I was also heavily involved in the design and implementation of the website's front-end (HTML/CSS).
